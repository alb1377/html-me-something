I enjoy creating and learning to make things. Creating art, for me, provides an outlet. I find it relaxing and challenging. Therefore, I would like to relay a little bit of what I enjoy, and what sort of things I prefer using or would like to try.

Art Mediums

There are a variety of art mediums available to choose from for the beginner or the advanced artist. I usually work with canvas, watercolor paper, or sketchpad for my drawings. I'd really like to get into working with wood as a medium, on both a large and scale.

Drawing

In most art projects, you are typically lightly sketching your subject/object onto to your art medium. I very much enjoy drawing in pencil and charcoal. I draw on a smaller scale more often than not. I started out creating abstract works, but enjoy scenery sketches the most.

Painting

There are a variety of different kinds of paint you can use. Such as: Acrylic, Oil, and Watercolor. I like painting in acrylic and watercolor the most. I find myself more often painting with acrylic paint than anything else. I find it easier to maintain shapes and to layer to give it depth along with a wider color range. Watercolor I find challenging because its hard to maintain distinction between objects and to get more vibrant colors.
